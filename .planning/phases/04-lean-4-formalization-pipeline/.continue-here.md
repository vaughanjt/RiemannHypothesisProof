  barrier = sum_rho |H(rho)|^2 - C(L)

  sum_rho |H(rho)|^2 = manifestly >= 0 (sum of squares)
  C(L) = analytic correction (Gamma, conductor, NO primes)

TO PROVE barrier > 0:
  Show sum_rho |H(rho)|^2 > C(L) for all L.

The spectral sum DIVERGES (grows with more zeros).

So with enough zeros, spectral always wins.

  For each L, the first K(L) zeros contribute > C(L).
  K(L) must be bounded or the argument must work uniformly.
