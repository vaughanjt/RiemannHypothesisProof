## H_t(z) Computation
- H_0(z) = xi(1/2+iz) via mpmath — validated to <1e-18 at 5 known zeta zeros
- FFT heat evolution: hat{H_t}(k) = exp(-tk^2) hat{H_0}(k)
- H_t profiles plotted at t = 0, 0.01, 0.02, 0.05, 0.1

## Zero Dynamics ODE
- Coulomb ODE: dz_j/dt = +2 sum_{k!=j} 1/(z_j - z_k)
- Vectorized RHS (N^2 matrix ops), DOP853 solver
- Forward evolution (t: 0->0.1): repulsion confirmed, min gap grows 1.38x
- Backward evolution (t: 0->-0.08): attraction confirmed, min gap shrinks to 42%

## Key Results
- Spacing variance crosses GUE (0.178) near t ~ 0.08 forward

- Closest pair (z ~ 111) gap shrinks linearly backward, extrapolated collision ~ t=-0.15
- Small-gap fraction doubles backward (8% -> 16%), halves forward (8% -> 2%)
