<context>
The barrier = <w, QW, w> = (W02-Mp) - M_diag - M_alpha is the Rayleigh quotient
of the Weil quadratic form on the odd eigenvector of W02. Under RH, QW >= 0,
so barrier >= 0 always. The question is whether it approaches 0 or stays bounded
away from 0 as lam^2 -> infinity.

W02-Mp converges to 3.01 (solid numerical evidence from 14 data points).
M_diag+M_alpha is 2.74 at lam^2=10000 and growing. If it exceeds 3.01,
the barrier goes negative — which would either falsify RH or indicate an
error in our formulation. Analytical derivation of M_diag asymptotics is needed.
</context>
