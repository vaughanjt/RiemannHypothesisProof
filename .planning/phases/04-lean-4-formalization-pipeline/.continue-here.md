## Gamma-Zeta Decomposition
- smooth_gamma(s) = log(1/2) + log(s) - (s/2)*log(pi) + log Gamma(s/2)
- log_sz(s) = log((s-1)*zeta(s))  [encodes prime distribution]
- Gamma part: negative for n=1..7, positive for n>=8, grows like (n/2)*ln(n)
- Zeta part: oscillates, NEGATIVE at n=156..186 (min -1.026 at n=172)
- Total always positive (Gamma dominates)

## Key Findings
- Tightest constraint: n=1 with 4.2% safety margin
- Stieltjes constants validated: d_1 = gamma (Euler constant), d_k alternating/decaying
- Oscillation period ~89, matches 2*pi/arg(1-1/rho_1) from gamma_1=14.13
- M-function connection: Li coefficients average M-function over circle around s=1
- Per-zero contributions: each zero contributes on unit circle in generating function variable

## Critical Insight
- The M-function (Session 16) and Li criterion test the SAME positivity in different bases
- M-function: pointwise in s (uncountable constraints)
- Li criterion: discrete modes n=1,2,3,... (countable constraints)
- The Li basis averages local M-function oscillations
